Poem of the Times

I first started Poem of the Times back around 2003, and for about five years I harvested a poem each day, from the top headlines, of the top english speaking newspapers around the world.

Sometime in 2008 I stopped generating the poem each day, but in an effort to showcase the potential of APIs, I thought it would be a good time to revive the project--so here we go, with a Poem of the Times reboot.
